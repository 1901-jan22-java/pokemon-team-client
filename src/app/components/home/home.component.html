Understanding Pokemone Types Strength and Weaknesses

Pokemon Types Types are properties for Pokemon and their moves. Currently as of the most recent generation of pokemon, we have 18 types. Some people referred to types also as as elements.

Strength and Weaknessses in Types:

  • We refer to as 'Type Match-Ups'.
  • Strength: This is where certain Types can deal either Super Effective damage.
  • Weakness: Not Very Effective damage, or no damage whatsoever to another Type depending on what Move you use
  • Goal: we are constantly on the lookout for the best possible Type Match-Up in order to claim victory over our opponent!

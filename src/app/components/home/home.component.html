Understanding Pokemone Types Strength and Weaknesses

Pokemon Types Types are properties for Pokemon and their moves. Currently as of the most recent generation of pokemon, we have 18 types. Some people referred to types also as as elements.

Strength and Weaknessses in Types:

  • We refer to as 'Type Match-Ups'.
  • Strength: This is where certain Types can deal either Super Effective damage.
  • Weakness: Not Very Effective damage, or no damage whatsoever to another Type depending on what Move you use
  • Goal: we are constantly on the lookout for the best possible Type Match-Up in order to claim victory over our opponent!
In Depth Guide For Strength and Weaknesses in Type

Normal Type

Normal is weak to fighting. Normal gives and recieves neutral damage to everything except for rock and steel which resist it, and ghost.  Normal is immune to ghost and can't damage it either. 

Grass Type

Grass is weak to fire, flying, bug, posion, and ice. Grass resists water, electricity, grass, and ground.  Grass is super effective on water, rock, and ground.

Fire Type

Fire is weak to water, ground, and rock. Fire is super effective on grass, bug, ice, and steel. Fire resists grass, fire, steel, ice, and fairy.

Water Type

Water is weak to grass and electric.  Water is super effective on fire, rock, and ground.   Water resists fire, steel, water, and ice.

Fighting Type

Fighting is weak to psychic, fairy, and flying.  Fighting is super effective on rock, steel, ice, dark, and normal.   Fighting resists rock, bug, and dark.

Flying Type

Flying is weak to electric, rock, and ice.  Flying is super effective on bug, grass, and fighting.  Flying resists fighting and grass.  Flying is immune to ground.

Poison Type

Poison is weak to ground and psychic.  Poison is super effective on fairy and grass.  Poison resists fighting, poison, bug, grass, and fairy.  Poison has no effect on steel. Ground is weak to water, grass, and ice.  Ground is super effective on electric, fire, rock, steel, and poison.  Ground resists poison, and rock.  Ground is immune to electric.

Rock Type

Rock is weak to fighting, ground, water, grass, and steel. Rock is super effective on fire, flying, ice, and bug.  Rock resists normal, flying, fire, and poison.

Bug Type

Bug is weak to flying, rock, and fire.  Bug is super effective on grass, dark, and psychic.  Bug resists grass, fighting, and ground.

Ghost Type

Ghost is weak to ghost and dark.  Ghost is super effective on psychic and ghost.   Ghost resists poison and bug.  Ghost is immune to normal and fighting.   Ghost has no effect on normal.

Steel Type

Steel is weak to fire, fighting, and ground.  Steel is super effective on rock, ice, and fairy.  Steel resists normal, flying, rock, bug, steel, grass, psychic, ice, dragon, and fairy.  Steel is immune to poison.

NElectric Type

Electric is weak to ground.  Electric is super effective on water and flying.  Electric resists flying, electric, and steel.  Electric has no effect on ground.

Psychic Type

Psychic is weak to bug, ghost, and dark.   Psychic is super effective on poison, and fighting.  Psychic resists psychic and fighting.  Psychic has no effect on dark.

Fire Type

Ice is weak to fire, fighting, steel, and rock.   Ice is super effective on dragon, grass, ground, and flying.  Ice resists ice.

Dragon Type

Dragon is weak to dragon, ice, and fairy.   Dragon is super effective on dragon.  Dragon resists fire, water, grass, and electric.   Dragon has no effect on fairy

Dark Type

Dark is weak to fighting and bug.  Dark is super effective on psychic and ghost.  Dark resists ghost and dark.  Dark is immune to psychic.

Fairy Type

Fairy is weak to steel and poison.  Fairy is super effective on dragon, dark, and fighting.  Fairy resists dark, fighting, and bug.  Fairy is immune to dragon.
